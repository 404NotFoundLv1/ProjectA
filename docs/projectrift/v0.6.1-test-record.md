Date: 2026-07-15

## Implemented scope

- Added `UPRWeaponDataAsset`, `UPRWeaponComponent`, `APRWeaponActor`, `UPRWeaponHUDWidget`, `DA_TestRifle`, and `DA_RifleAmmo`.
- Added one replicated primary slot with atomic equip/replace/unequip, a 12-round magazine, 48-round initial reserve, manual partial reload, accepted-shot ammo consumption, dry fire, fire-rate enforcement, and invalid-state rejection.
- Reworked `UGA_PrimaryAttack` to use the weapon component and the v0.6.0 unified GAS physical damage pipeline.
- Added server-side camera/control-rotation aiming followed by a muzzle trace against world and pawn object types, preventing third-person camera shots through near-muzzle cover.
- Added local ADS camera interpolation, replicated aim/reload state, native crosshair/ammo feedback, inventory equipment controls, and GAS debug weapon state.
- Added `IA_Aim` and `IA_Reload` to `IMC_Default`; R is reserved for reload, while primary fire retains left mouse and gains right trigger.
- Extended v4 runtime snapshots and multiplayer projections with `Slot.Primary` and normalized total ammunition. Save schema remains v4.
- Added idempotent starter provisioning. Unknown legacy primary equipment remains intact and receives the rifle in the backpack.

No external art, save-version migration, secondary weapon, weapon switching, recoil, projectile ballistics, penetration, headshots, audio, VFX, GameplayCue, reload animation, or automatic reload was added.
