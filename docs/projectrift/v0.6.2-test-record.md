# ProjectRift v0.6.2 test record

Date: 2026-07-16  
Branch: `main`

## Implemented scope

- Added structured `FPRDamageRequest` and `FPRResolvedDamage` contracts over the v0.6.0 Damage Execution without changing its formulas.
- Added exact shield/health settlement feedback, shield-break/lethal classification, preserved `FHitResult`, original-instigator attribution, and fail-closed public post-settlement dispatch.
- Added Light/Heavy hit stagger with refresh/priority rules, stun supersession, ability/ADS/reload cancellation, movement restoration, and downed/dead cleanup.
- Wired rifle Light 0.12 seconds, enemy melee Heavy 0.30 seconds, assault-blast Heavy presentation plus existing stun, and pollution with no stagger or hit confirmation.
- Added four impact and three persistent status GameplayCues, a shared feedback component, native Cue notify base, ProjectRift Cue assets, an overlay material, and a lightweight impact particle.
- Added source-owner-only unreliable hit confirmation, actual split damage numbers, local hit direction, and camera shake scaled to 35% by the existing v4 reduced-shake setting.
- Updated GAS/enemy debug text, `GameplayCueNotifyPaths`, and the displayed project version. Save schema remains v4.

No audio, production art, recoil, hit-location multipliers, poise, armor, critical hits, penetration, persistence migration, or client damage prediction was added.
